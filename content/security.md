## 1. Our Commitment to Security

At Sumartian Studios, we are committed to maintaining the security and integrity of our software projects, websites, and user data. Security is a fundamental aspect of our development practices, and we take all reports of security vulnerabilities seriously.

## 2. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices in our development processes, including:

- Regular code reviews and security audits
- Use of secure coding practices and frameworks
- Timely application of security patches and updates
- Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
- Access controls and authentication mechanisms

## 3. Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

We follow a coordinated vulnerability disclosure process in accordance with industry best practices. If you discover a security vulnerability in any of our projects or services, we ask that you:

- Report the vulnerability promptly to our security team
- Provide sufficient detail to allow us to reproduce and address the issue
- Avoid public disclosure until we have had adequate time to investigate and resolve the issue
- Refrain from exploiting the vulnerability or accessing unauthorized data

## 4. Reporting Security Issues

To report a security vulnerability, please contact our security team through the following methods:

- Email: [security@sumartian-studios.github.io](mailto:security@sumartian-studios.github.io)
- Use our [contact form](/contact/) with "Security Issue" in the subject line

Please include the following information in your report:

- A clear description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Potential impact and severity assessment
- Your contact information for follow-up

## 5. Response Process

Upon receiving a security report, we will:

- Acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
- Investigate the reported issue promptly
- Provide regular updates on our progress
- Coordinate disclosure timing with the reporter
- Publish security advisories and patches as appropriate

## 6. Bug Bounty Program

While we do not currently offer a formal bug bounty program, we greatly appreciate security researchers who help improve our security posture. We may provide recognition or other forms of compensation for significant findings at our discretion.

## 7. Security Best Practices for Users

To protect yourself when using our software or services:

- Keep your systems and software up to date
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication where available
- Be cautious with third-party integrations
- Regularly backup your data
- Monitor for unusual activity

## 8. Disclaimer

While we strive to maintain high security standards, no system is completely immune to vulnerabilities. Our software is provided "as is" without warranties of any kind. Users are responsible for assessing and managing their own security risks when using our projects.

## 9. Contact Information

For security-related inquiries or to report vulnerabilities, please use the contact methods listed above. For general support or other questions, visit our [contact page](/contact/).

## 10. Updates to This Policy

This security policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post any updates on this page.

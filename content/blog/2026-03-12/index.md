When I originally started working on Invictus, I quickly ran into a problem: natural language processing. From the outset, Artificial Intelligence was intended to be an integral part of the visualization system. However, during that period, large language models had not yet achieved widespread adoption or maturity, making the intended integration technically unfeasible.

Given these constraints, the project was temporarily suspended pending advancements in the field. With the rapid evolution of AI technology and the emergence of robust, accessible LLM infrastructure, the technical prerequisites have now been met. Development has resumed, positioning Invictus to fulfill its original vision with the necessary computational capabilities now available.
